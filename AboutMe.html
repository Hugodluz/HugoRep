ABOUT ME

HEAVY LIGHT VISUALS

Heavy Light Visuals is an independent graphic design studio founded in 2024. It is currently managed by a single person, its creator and founder, Hugo Gabriel da Luz, originally from Cape Verde and now based in Portugal. The goal of our studio is to explore our capabilities in the field of graphic design, continuously improving with each project, always aiming to leave our clients satisfied. Today, our studio operates in various fields, including music, sports, and the corporate world. However, we have recently seen higher demand from athletes, resulting in more work being produced for the sports industry.
